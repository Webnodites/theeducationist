The Educationist Society

  • The purpose of the society/trust is the welfare of the society through Education, Employability, Entrepreneurship and Career Advisory & Counselling services.
  • To sponsor, promote, develop aid and run institutions, trusts, societies, school, teacher training institutes, educational institutions, libraries and other charitable societies.
  • To promote entrepreneur skills to unemployed youth and creation of self-employment opportunities for them.
  • To establish and run institute which shall be non-profit organizations devoted to education.
  • To provide scholarships and special facilities to financially deprived students irrespective of their religion, race, caste, creed, sex and place of birth or any of them.
  • To organize cultural shows, dramas, fetes and sports for the benefit of the society on non-commercial basis.
  • To organize lectures, talks, debates, seminars and to conduct surveys; to acquire and impart; the development of knowledge, skill, mind and character of students under normal educational conditions for the furtherance of process of improving education system in India.
  • To establish and run educational institutions including day boarding and boarding schools for boys and girls without distinction of religion, caste or creed and also to take over existing educational institutions for the above purpose.
  • To provide the restructuring of education, Pre-primary Primary & higher education, facilities in the fields of Medical, Engineering, Yoga, Sports, Agriculture & Horticulture, Management, Computer Science & in all Vocational Courses, any type of education courses benefiting people of the society, Primary & Pre-Primary education for needy students, intellectuals, & other persons of the Society belonging to any caste, creed and religion etc., and Trust operations can be anywhere in India and rest of the World.
  • To engage Teachers, Professors, Instructors, Professionals, Management Personnel, Technical Advisors and Experts of good moral character and having capabilities to impart efficiently and economically up-to-date knowledge to students in Medical, Engineering, Management, Computer Science, all Vocational Courses, Primary & Pre-Primary education, intellectual and other usual pursuits.
  • To establish, acquire, construct, maintain and run Colleges, Institutions, Polytechnics to provide all sorts of education in the fields of Medical, Engineering, Management, Arts, Yoga, Sports, Agriculture & Horticulture, Vocational Courses, Education in any field benefiting people of the Society, and also Primary education & Pre-Primary to the needy children.
  • To promote, spread and impart end to end, all aspects of Education at all levels from Pre-primary to K-12 Schools, Universities etc. all based on the concept of Human Mind Development and Integral Education as envisaged by the Trustees from time to time.
  • To engage in any activity in any field, someway resulting in the benefit of the people of the Society.
  • To establish, acquire, construct, maintain and run Auditorium, Sports Complex for furtherance of skills of Sports, athletics etc.
  • To open Community Centers for use by any member of the Society belonging to any caste, creed or religion etc.
  • To conceptualize, construct and run schools, colleges, and educational institutions for the benefit of the public at large.
  • To establish, maintain and run boarding houses, boarding schools and residential accommodations for students/staff/faculty and for those who are connected with the institutions.
  • To promote tie-ups with educational institutions from across the world and to grant research and / or financial assistance to any educational institution for carrying out research, granting scholarships, prizes, medals, awards for excellence in studies, sports and scientific research, distribution of books and note books for poor and or deserving students
  • To develop a healthy as well as critical attitude towards the development of mental, physical and moral upliftment of the students and those connected with the institutions so as to make them good citizens.
  • To accept donations, grants, presents and other offerings and to deal with the same for the purpose of the trust.
  • To charge moderate tuition fees and otherwise recoup themselves for the outlay and expenses incurred / to be incurred in upkeep and maintenance of institutions established or about to be established under this deed.
  • To train and equip pupils so as to be self - supporting in an honourable and decent way of life so as to develop as good, healthy and progressive citizens.
  • To develop discretionary conduct and habit to observe the rule of law and self-resistance.
  • To carry out, encourage and develop inventive and research facilities for pupils and teachers and to afford opportunity on research work in Medical Science, Art, Science, Industrial and Information Technology and other allied areas of these types.
  • To enter into any agreement or arrangement with any other Society, foundation, or any other party having any legal entity or any individual for the smooth functioning and achievements of the objectives of the Trust.
  • The trust is a public charitable institution and has not been started with a view to earn any profits; all income/s of the Trust and Institutions run by the Trust shall be utilized towards promotion of aims and objects of the Trust only. The Trust has been established to do all for the benefit of the general public, belonging to any sect, caste, creed or religion.
