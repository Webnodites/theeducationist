Overview

Many of our products are being made by the women who are the members of self help groups under the guidance of The Red Dot Foundation - a social service organisation of the women, for the women and by the women.

The Red Dot Foundation is already working in the areas of creating awareness through their campaign on the Importance of Mental, Physical and Emotional Health of Women, but by collaborating with ibex casuals, they are creating economic empowerment to strengthen the Financial Health of women as well.
